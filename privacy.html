Privacy Policy for Infinity Prompt

Last updated: February 06, 2026

Introduction

Infinity Prompt ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our browser extension.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data, browsing history, or personal details on our servers. The extension operates with a focus on privacy and data minimization.

User Input Data

When you use the "Optimize Prompt" or rewrite features, the text currently in your input field is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. This data is:

  • Transferred securely via HTTPS.
  • Used solely to generate the enhanced text response.
  • Not stored by us (the developer) after the request is completed.

Local Settings

We store your preferences (such as the list of domains where the extension is hidden) locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

We use the Google Gemini API as our AI provider to process your text requests. By using this extension, you acknowledge that your input text is processed by Google's generative AI services. Google's data handling is governed by their own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Usage

We certify that:

  1. We do not sell user data to third parties.
  2. We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension's core functionality (text enhancement).
  3. We do not use user data for creditworthiness or lending purposes.
